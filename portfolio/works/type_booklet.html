Suddnely a Mirror

This American Life: Episode 860 Suddenly A Mirror

This was my American Life podcast book for episode 860, created during my Advanced Typography class. The instructions for the assignment were to create a printed booklet using only type, with the assistance of shapes and colors, but no clear illustrations other than on the covers. I wanted to create something at was all out punk and get crazy with it. Often, because of my job, I focus so much on the readability and the style guide rules, I don't have a chance to break out and create something I find super cool. This was my chance to do that, and I took it.
