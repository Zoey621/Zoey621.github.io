Game Week

For my College-Level Publication Design course, we were tasked with creating a publication for whatever we wanted. I decided to create a game week publication for the NMU hockey team. I created a cover, a senior night spread, and two spreads for the regular season games against Lawrence Tech and Michigan Tech. I also created a mockup of the cover on a phone to show how it would look in digital format.
