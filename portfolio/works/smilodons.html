PWHL Smilodon

For my College-Level Logo and Identity Design course, we were told to create a full identity and brand guide for whatever we wanted. I decided to create a fictional PWHL team for Detroit called the Smilodons. Smilodon is the scientific name for saber sabretooth tiger. Since there are already the Sabers in the NHL, I felt this was a fun way to keep the cats in Detroit without copying an already existing team's name. I was required to create four posters and one short motion graphic for what is displayed here, and mockups to go along with each poster.
