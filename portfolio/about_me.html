Zoey Lawrence

Hi! It's so wonderful you want to know more about me!

I am a Senior Designer who currently focuses on multimedia products, from newspapers to sports graphics. I love learning about different graphic styles and incorporating them wherever I can. One of my biggest influences is the different grunge and punk styles, more specifically Jamie Reid.

Being part of student news is something I take extreme pride in and want to showcase anytime I'm given the chance. Having an opportunity to elevate people's stories with the power of design is something I wouldn't trade for the world.Currently, I am a pursuing a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Graphic Design,a second Bacholors of Applied Arts in Animation, with a minor in Religion.
